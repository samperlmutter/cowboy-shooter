LONESOME RIDER
By Team Green Shorts
Chris Mineweaser, Kristin Pflug, Sam Perlmutter, and Don Robinson

INSTRUCTIONS
-Use the arrow keys to move around
-Press the space bar to shoot
-Defeat the all enemies in the train car to progress forward towards the front of the train
-Once you reach the front of the train, defeat the Engineer boss to win the game
-Press G to restart the game or M to return to the main menu

CHEAT CODES
Press K to instantly drop player's health to 5
Press H to increase player's health by 50
Press E to instantly move the player to the end of the first level
Press B to instantly move to the boss level
Press P to add 10 points to the player's score
